My Background

A journey from Lebanon to leadership in IT

1982 – Immigration

Born in Lebanon, immigrated to the United States due to the war and instability in the region.

1984 – High School

Graduated from East Surry High School in Pilot Mountain, NC.

NC State University

Initially majored in Mechanical Engineering, later discovered a strength in logic and switched to Computer Science.

First Job – Carolina Freight Carrier

Worked as a Mainframe Developer for 5 years until the company was acquired by ABF, leading to the data center closing.

SIECOR / Corning

Joined as a developer for corporate and plant systems, later moving into data warehousing and database administration.

PPD Career

Joined PPD as a Database Administrator, promoted to Manager and later Senior Manager, leading global teams and IT strategy.
